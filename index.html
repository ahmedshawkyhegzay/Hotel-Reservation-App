King Khufu

king Egyptian pharaoh

It is the birth name of the ancient Egyptian pharaoh, who ruled during the Fourth Dynasty of the Old Kingdom around 2580 BC.Pharaoh Khufu, the second king of the Fourth Dynasty, who followed Pharaoh Snefru on the throne and who is likely to be his father. It is well established that his mother was Queen Hetepheres, whose tomb is located in the Giza cemetery. He is credited with building the Great Pyramid on the Giza Plateau as a tomb for him, one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World.
